What We Offer

Design Services
Tailored to Your Life

Space Planning

We map your rooms with purpose — studying how you move, gather, and rest to create layouts that work effortlessly for daily life.

Full Interior Design

From concept moodboards through final installation, we manage every detail — materials, finishes, lighting, and furnishings — to deliver a cohesive whole.

Furniture & Sourcing

We curate pieces from trusted craftspeople and makers worldwide — or design bespoke furniture specific to your space when nothing off-the-shelf will do.

Lighting Design

Light shapes perception more than any other element. We layer ambient, task, and accent sources to sculpt atmosphere across every hour of the day.

Renovation Consulting

Planning a remodel? We guide you through decisions before a single wall moves — so contractors get clear briefs and budgets stay on track.

Commercial Spaces

Offices, hospitality, and retail environments designed to reflect your brand and support the people who inhabit them every day.

About the Studio

Designing Spaces
That Inspire Living

Living Acre was founded on the belief that good design isn't a luxury — it's a necessity. Our studio brings together architects, decorators, and craftspeople who share one obsession: creating interiors that feel genuinely inhabited and deeply personal.

We work with a small number of clients each year so that every project receives the time and attention it deserves.
